How Signal Board Works

A highly-engineered process designed to turn collaborative chaos into structured, executable reality.

1

Canvas Provisioning

Instantiates an infinite, hardware-accelerated workspace. Engineered to handle thousands of simultaneous objects—from text to complex vectors—without dropping a single frame of performance.

2

Multiplayer Sync

Utilizes advanced WebSockets to render live cursors, active typing indicators, and object movements from remote team members globally, operating with true zero latency.

3

Media Ingestion

A frictionless drop-zone. Seamlessly process dragged-and-dropped high-resolution images, generate instant sticky notes, embed live videos, and parse raw code snippets natively on the canvas.

4

Action Item Extraction

Closes the loop. Automatically groups clustered sticky notes and ideas, allowing users to instantly convert a brainstorming session into structured, assignable tasks within Signal Plan.
